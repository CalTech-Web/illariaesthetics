## Services Offered

Services should appear in this order: Skin Care, Injectables, Weight Loss, IV Therapy, Products.

### 1. Skin Care
- URL: `/skin-care`
- Primary SEO keyword focus: Chicago physician-led skin care / Dr. Chavez as a doctor doing medical-grade treatments
- **Skin Survey** - Full facial survey of all facial lesions, scars, and blemishes with treatment options, recommendations, and pricing. Purpose: professional opinion for clients unsure of their needs or seeking a second opinion.
- **Cryoablation** - Uses extremely cold liquid to freeze and destroy abnormal tissue. Targets hyperpigmentation, sun spots, benign skin tissue, melasma, and irregular pigmentation.
- **Surgical Dermatology** - Removal of warts, suspicious or benign lesions, and moles with minimal scarring. Also addresses scar removal and revisions.
- **Chemical Peels** - Customizable to any peel history. Improves skin tone and pigmentation, targets acne, and reduces pore size. Doctor quote: "As a doctor, we use higher grade medical products for a better patient result."
- **Dermaplane** - Manual exfoliation removing surface debris, increasing topical product absorption, creating a healthier and more radiant appearance. Pairs well with a facial or chemical peel.
- **Microneedling** - Promotes collagen and elastin production. Creates small controlled injuries in the first layer of skin to keep skin firm and smooth. Good for texture, acne, and exfoliation.
- **Note:** Remove Signature Facial from this page entirely.

### 2. Injectables
- URL: `/injectables`
- Primary SEO keyword focus: Chicago injectables / Botox Chicago / physician-administered injectables
- **Wrinkle Relaxers** - Relax muscles and temporarily reduce the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles. Brands used: Botox, Xeomin, and Dysport.
- **Filler** - Hyaluronic Acid product that restores volume to the face, smooths fine lines and wrinkles, and creates fuller, plumper lips.
- **PRF (Platelet Rich Fibrinogen)** - Natural option derived from the patient's own blood (1-2 vials drawn and spun at lower speed to separate PRF from red blood cells). Used for skin rejuvenation, hair growth promotion, or wound healing aid. Injected back into the body.

### 3. Weight Loss
- URL: `/weight-loss-1`
- Primary SEO keyword focus: Chicago medical weight loss / GLP-1 Chicago / physician-supervised weight loss
- **Program:** GLP-1 receptor agonist program (tirzepatide and semaglutide) with personalized support
- **Includes:** Monthly provider check-ins, tailored medication regimens, regular weight assessments
- **Cost:** Starting around $350/month
- **Candidates:** BMI of 27 or greater, or those who have struggled with diet and exercise alone
- **FAQ content:**

  - How do GLP-1s work? Target receptors in brain and GI tract; mimic natural GLP-1 hormone; regulate appetite, slow gastric emptying, promote fullness; increase energy expenditure; improve insulin sensitivity

## FAQ Content

**Q: What makes Illari Aesthetics different from other med spas in Chicago?**
Illari Aesthetics is led by Dr. Milton Chavez, a board-certified family physician with over 30 years of clinical experience. As a doctor, Dr. Chavez uses higher-grade medical products and evidence-based protocols for better patient results. Every treatment is customized to your individual skin type and goals, not templated.

**Q: What injectables do you offer?**
We offer wrinkle relaxers including Botox, Xeomin, and Dysport, as well as hyaluronic acid dermal fillers for volume restoration and lip enhancement, and PRF (Platelet Rich Fibrinogen) treatments using your own blood for natural skin rejuvenation and hair growth support.

**Q: What skin care treatments do you offer?**
We offer skin surveys, cryoablation, surgical dermatology (lesion and mole removal, scar revision), chemical peels, dermaplaning, and microneedling. All treatments use medical-grade products from AlumierMD and Derma Made.

**Q: What is your weight loss program?**
Our program combines GLP-1 receptor agonists (tirzepatide or semaglutide) with monthly provider check-ins, tailored medication dosing, and regular weight assessments. It is a holistic, physician-supervised approach for sustainable results. Plans start around $350 per month.

**Q: Am I a candidate for the weight loss program?**
GLP-1 programs are suitable for individuals with a BMI of 27 or greater, or those who have struggled with weight loss through diet and exercise alone. Schedule a consultation to determine if the program is right for you.

**Q: What IV therapy options do you offer?**
We offer IV infusions and micronutrient injections targeting beauty, energy, immunity, hangover recovery, allergy relief, NAD+ anti-aging, migraine relief, and more. Infusions take approximately 45 minutes; injections take approximately 15 minutes.

**Q: What skincare products do you carry?**
We carry AlumierMD and Derma Made, two professional-grade skincare lines used and recommended by Dr. Chavez and our staff. AlumierMD products can also be purchased online through our referral link. We chose these products to complement the services we offer and the recommendations we give.

**Q: How do I book an appointment?**
You can book online at illariaesthetics.com/schedule or call us at 773.219.0326. We are open Monday through Friday, 9am to 4pm.

## Key Messaging / Brand Voice

- **Physician-led, not just esthetician-run:** The core differentiator is Dr. Chavez being a board-certified doctor. Every piece of copy should reinforce that this is a medical practice, not a typical beauty spa.
- **Medical-grade results:** The word "medical-grade" applies to both products (AlumierMD, Derma Made) and procedures. This language justifies premium positioning.
- **Personalized care:** Illari does not use cookie-cutter treatments. Every patient gets a customized plan based on their skin type and goals.
- **Natural enhancement:** The mission is to "facilitate the expression of your true desired appearance" and to "enhance and rejuvenate one's natural beauty" not to alter identity.
- **Chicago / Wicker Park identity:** The location in Wicker Park is a brand asset. It signals a sophisticated, community-focused practice in a culturally vibrant Chicago neighborhood.
- **Welcoming and professional:** The spa atmosphere is designed to be "both welcoming and professional" so patients feel comfortable and confident.
- **Comprehensive wellness:** Illari is not just aesthetics. IV therapy and weight loss programs position the brand as a whole-body wellness destination.
- **Tone:** Warm, knowledgeable, and confident. Not clinical or cold. Not overly salesy. Write as a trusted doctor and wellness partner, not a beauty brand.
